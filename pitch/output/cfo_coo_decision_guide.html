EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The business model is sound: Y1 profit 876K DKK, Y2 profit 4.3M DKK. However, a bootstrapped launch (0 DKK starting cash) creates an Aug-Sep funding gap of ~50-100K DKK. This workshop must resolve four categories of decisions before signing the Contribution Agreement.

Cash goes negative in Aug, bottoms at -50.347 DKK in Sep, then recovers sharply in Oct when revenue ramps. The gap is manageable (~100K buffer needed) but requires a decision on funding source.

Questions to Decide

Q1: Who provides the bridge capital? - Option A: Daniel founder loan — Daniel provides 100K DKK as convertible note or loan. Repaid from Oct+ profits. Adds to his risk exposure. - Option B: Deferred compensation — Daniel takes 50% salary in Aug-Sep (80K saved). Creates personal cash flow pressure but manageable. - Option C: Client pre-payment — Negotiate 50% upfront on Sep-Oct workshops. Requires strong early relationships. - Option D: External seed — 100K from angel/family. Dilutes founding equity. - Option E: Combination — 50K loan + 50K deferred comp. Spreads risk.

Q2: What triggers repayment? - If founder loan: When does principal repay? Interest rate? (Suggestion: Oct-Nov from first positive cash) - If deferred comp: When does Daniel get back-paid? (Suggestion: Dec-26 bonus from Y1 profit)

Q3: What happens if the gap is larger than modeled? - The model assumes 100% collection on time. What if a client pays 30 days late? - Buffer recommendation: Secure 100K with 50K contingency buffer.

CATEGORY 3: HIRING GATES (P2 — DEFINE TRIGGERS)

The model assumes Senior Hire + Account Manager both start Oct-26. This creates a 148% cost step change (125K → 310K/month). The hire gate must be clearly defined.

The Sep-15 Gate Review

Current assumption: If ≥2 Catalysts are signed by Sep-15, proceed with hires.

Questions to Decide: - Q15: Is "2 Catalysts signed" the right gate? What about "1 Catalyst + 8 workshops"? - Q16: Who makes the hire/no-hire call? Unanimous? Majority? Daniel alone? - Q17: What if we hit 1.5 Catalysts (one signed, one verbal)? Proceed or wait? - Q18: If we don't hire, what is Plan B for Oct-Dec? Daniel solo at reduced capacity?
