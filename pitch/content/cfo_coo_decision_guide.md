## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The business model is sound: Y1 profit 876K DKK, Y2 profit 4.3M DKK. However, a bootstrapped launch (0 DKK starting cash) creates an **Aug-Sep funding gap of ~50-100K DKK**. This workshop must resolve four categories of decisions before signing the Contribution Agreement.

## CATEGORY 1: FUNDING THE CASH GAP (P0 — MUST DECIDE TODAY)

### The Problem
| Month | Cumulative Cash | Status |
|-------|-----------------|--------|
| Apr-26 | 115.000 | OK |
| May-26 | 162.329 | OK |
| Jun-26 | 199.658 | Peak |
| Jul-26 | 36.991 | Low (holiday month) |
| **Aug-26** | **-25.678** | **NEGATIVE** |
| **Sep-26** | **-50.347** | **TROUGH** |
| Oct-26 | 198.978 | Recovery |
| Nov-26 | 394.971 | Strong |
| Dec-26 | 512.964 | Y1 close |

Cash goes negative in Aug, bottoms at **-50.347 DKK in Sep**, then recovers sharply in Oct when revenue ramps. The gap is manageable (~100K buffer needed) but requires a decision on funding source.

### Questions to Decide

**Q1: Who provides the bridge capital?**
- **Option A: Daniel founder loan** — Daniel provides 100K DKK as convertible note or loan. Repaid from Oct+ profits. Adds to his risk exposure.
- **Option B: Deferred compensation** — Daniel takes 50% salary in Aug-Sep (80K saved). Creates personal cash flow pressure but manageable.
- **Option C: Client pre-payment** — Negotiate 50% upfront on Sep-Oct workshops. Requires strong early relationships.
- **Option D: External seed** — 100K from angel/family. Dilutes founding equity.
- **Option E: Combination** — 50K loan + 50K deferred comp. Spreads risk.

**Q2: What triggers repayment?**
- If founder loan: When does principal repay? Interest rate? (Suggestion: Oct-Nov from first positive cash)
- If deferred comp: When does Daniel get back-paid? (Suggestion: Dec-26 bonus from Y1 profit)

**Q3: What happens if the gap is larger than modeled?**
- The model assumes 100% collection on time. What if a client pays 30 days late?
- Buffer recommendation: Secure 100K with 50K contingency buffer.

## CATEGORY 3: HIRING GATES (P2 — DEFINE TRIGGERS)

The model assumes Senior Hire + Account Manager both start Oct-26. This creates a **148% cost step change** (125K → 310K/month). The hire gate must be clearly defined.

### The Sep-15 Gate Review

**Current assumption:** If ≥2 Catalysts are signed by Sep-15, proceed with hires.

**Questions to Decide:**
- **Q15:** Is "2 Catalysts signed" the right gate? What about "1 Catalyst + 8 workshops"?
- **Q16:** Who makes the hire/no-hire call? Unanimous? Majority? Daniel alone?
- **Q17:** What if we hit 1.5 Catalysts (one signed, one verbal)? Proceed or wait?
- **Q18:** If we don't hire, what is Plan B for Oct-Dec? Daniel solo at reduced capacity?

### Senior Hire Risk

- 3-month ramp before the hire can lead workshops
- First solo delivery: ~Jan-27
- If hire fails (quits, underperforms), we've spent 300K with nothing to show

**Questions to Decide:**
- **Q19:** What is the Senior Hire profile? Must-haves vs nice-to-haves?
- **Q20:** Who leads the hiring process? Daniel? All three?
- **Q21:** What is the 90-day success metric for the Senior Hire?

### Account Manager Risk

- Replaces Henrik as primary closer from Oct-26
- Full commission model (45K base + 10% of revenue)
- If AM underperforms, Q4 sales collapse

**Questions to Decide:**
- **Q22:** What is AM ramp expectation? Closes in month 1? Month 2?
- **Q23:** Henrik to AM handoff — what does Henrik owe during transition?
- **Q24:** If AM fails, does Henrik step back in?
